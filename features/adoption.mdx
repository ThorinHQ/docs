The **Adoption** dashboard gives admins and managers a single view of how Thorin is being used across the organization. It's the right place to answer questions like "is our rollout working?", "which teams are getting the most out of agents?", and "where should we invest next?"

## Open the dashboard

Open **Adoption** from the side nav. You need at least manager-level access on at least one project to see the page; admins see org-wide totals.

## Time range

Use the range toggle at the top to switch between **7d**, **30d**, **90d**, and **all-time** windows. Every chart on the page recomputes against the selected window.

## What's in it

| Section | What it tells you |
| --- | --- |
| **Active users** | Unique people who chatted with an agent or completed a work item in the window. |
| **Sessions over time** | Trend chart of agent sessions, bucketed by day or week depending on range. |
| **Top skills** | The skills users invoke most often. |
| **Sessions by project** | Which [projects](/concepts/projects) are driving usage. |
| **Retention** | Cohort-style view of how many users keep coming back week-over-week. |
| **User leaderboard** | Top users by sessions completed. |

## Use it to drive rollouts

Common patterns:

- **Spotting a power team.** Sort the project leaderboard and look at what skills they install — those are candidates to roll out org-wide.
- **Finding stuck users.** Filter the user leaderboard for low session counts and reach out with a quickstart or training session.
- **Validating a new responsibility.** After publishing a new shared responsibility, watch the sessions trend for the next week.

Adoption data is read-only and refreshes every few minutes.
