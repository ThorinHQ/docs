---
title: "Gong, Salesforce, and Affinity"
description: "Connect revenue and deal systems for account, call, and deal workflows."
---

Gong, Salesforce, and Affinity are common inputs for deal flow and account workflows. Together they let agents combine conversations, CRM state, company information, relationship context, and next step history.

## Gong

Admins connect Gong with an access key and access key secret. Thorin validates the credentials and creates a Gong source resource.

Agents can search transcript data and read full call transcripts when granted access. This supports:

- Call summaries.
- Deal prep briefs.
- Quote extraction.
- Follow up generation.
- Opportunity timeline reconstruction.

## Salesforce

Thorin supports native Salesforce credentials and Salesforce Hosted MCP patterns, depending on workspace setup.

Native Salesforce setup commonly requires:

- Consumer key.
- Consumer secret.
- My Domain.
- Sandbox flag when applicable.
- API version.

Agents with Salesforce capability can look up accounts, contacts, opportunities, call records, opportunity history, and timelines when credentials are configured.

## Affinity

Admins connect Affinity with an organization API key. Agents can use Affinity to resolve:

- Deal status.
- Organization information.
- Relationship strength.

Affinity is useful as a secondary source when email or internal data is ambiguous, but it may lag behind live inbox context.

## Common combined workflow

For a deal or account prep workflow, an agent might:

1. Identify the account or opportunity.
2. Read Salesforce state and recent opportunity history.
3. Search Gong calls for recent conversations and objections.
4. Check Affinity relationship strength or deal status.
5. Read Notion or Drive battlecards.
6. Produce a concise brief, open questions, and recommended next actions.

## Caveats

- Gong and Salesforce metadata may not always reflect call time truth.
- Recent or internal only calls may not be synced.
- CRM fields can be incomplete or stale.
- Affinity should not override fresher email or direct customer context without explanation.
- Sensitive actions should create drafts or work items for review before sending.
