Trust Center is the admin surface for everything that controls what Thorin reads, who can do what, and what changed. It lives at **Admin → Trust Center** in the sidebar and is only visible to users with the admin role.

## Tabs

Trust Center splits into three tabs.

### Users

Per-user configuration for everyone in the tenant. The list shows each member, their role, and tenant membership at a glance. Click a row to open a detail panel with deeper settings — including the user's personal agent, connected mailboxes, and any per-user policy overrides.

Use the Users tab when you need to:

- Inspect or adjust a single teammate's setup.
- See a user's personal agent without switching accounts.
- Change a user's role or remove them from the tenant.

### Email Access

Inbox mode, allowlist, and deny rules. This is where you decide which mailboxes Thorin reads and which messages it skips.

The full reference lives at [Email access](/admin/email-access).

#### Privileged content protection

A toggle in the Email Access tab. When enabled, Thorin runs incoming messages through a classifier and skips anything labelled privileged — legal, HR, medical, and similarly sensitive content. The skipped messages never enter Thorin's processing pipeline.

This is the safest default for tenants that mix executive and operational mail in the same inboxes.

### Audit Logs

Every mutating action by a user, agent, or system actor lands here. Filter by:

- **Actor type** — user, agent, or system.
- **Specific actor** — a member picker for the chosen type.
- **Resource type** — agent, integration, automation, policy, and so on.
- **Free-text query** — searches across summaries and resource ids.

The table uses infinite scroll, so older entries load as you scroll down.

See [Audit logs](/admin/audit-logs) for the full reference.
