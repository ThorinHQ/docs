The **Admin → Control Panel** is the admin-only home for tenant configuration that doesn't fit cleanly into Integrations or Email Access. You need the `ADMIN` role to open it.

## Sections

### User config

The user-config tab lists every user in the tenant with their current authorization role and any per-user overrides. Use it to:

- See who is currently a `MEMBER`, `MANAGER`, or `ADMIN`.
- Apply an override (for example, force a user into read-only mode) without changing their WorkOS group membership.
- Revoke an override later.

Roles themselves are still mapped from WorkOS groups — see [Roles](/admin/roles). Overrides are an escape hatch for special cases.

### Email access

A shortcut into [Email access policies](/admin/email-access). The Control Panel surface gives you a quick at-a-glance summary of how many allowed inboxes and deny rules are active.

### Audit logs

A chronological feed of admin actions across the tenant:

- Role changes and overrides.
- Integration connect/disconnect events.
- Email access policy changes.
- MCP server adds and removals.

Filter by actor, action type, or date range. Logs are retained per your tenant's data-retention policy.

<Tip>
  Pull audit logs before any compliance review. They're the canonical record of who configured what.
</Tip>
