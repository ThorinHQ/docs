<Update label="June 18, 2026" tags={["launch", "docs"]}>
  ## Thorin documentation is live

  The public documentation site is now available. It covers the current product end to end, including agents, skills, automations, integrations, and admin controls.

  ### New

  - **Personal and project agents.** Two agent types are documented in detail: a [personal agent](/agents/personal-agent) that acts as your delegate, and [project agents](/agents/project-agents) that own shared team workflows. See [agent topology](/agents/topology) for how they relate.
  - **Skills.** Reusable, named procedures that agents can run. Learn what they are in the [skills overview](/skills/overview) and how to author your own in [Create skills](/skills/create-skills).
  - **Automations.** Run agents on [schedules and triggers](/automations/schedules-and-triggers) without a person in the loop. See the [automations overview](/automations/overview) for the full model.
  - **Workflow guides.** First-class write-ups of the workflows Thorin ships with: [deal flow](/workflows/deal-flow), [deal prep briefs](/workflows/deal-prep-briefs), [switchboard](/workflows/switchboard), and [work tracking](/workflows/work-tracking).
  - **Integrations.** Setup and security notes for [Slack](/integrations/slack), [Google Workspace](/integrations/google-workspace), [Notion](/integrations/notion), [Gong, Salesforce, and Affinity](/integrations/gong-salesforce-affinity), plus [MCP](/integrations/mcp) and [personal MCP connections](/integrations/personal-mcps).
  - **Knowledge and data.** Documented model for [resources](/resources/overview), [email feeds and entities](/resources/email-feeds-and-entities), the [library](/resources/library-and-entities), and [data sources](/resources/data-sources).
  - **Admin and security.** End-to-end guides for [onboarding](/admin/onboarding), the [security model](/admin/security-model), [users, roles, and permissions](/admin/users-roles-and-permissions), and [email access and compliance](/admin/email-access-and-compliance).

  ### Getting started

  - New [user quickstart](/getting-started/user-quickstart) for your first session with a personal agent.
