---
title: "Introduction"
description: "Thorin turns your inbox into AI-powered work — screening, classification, and automated follow-through."
---

Thorin is an email-first work platform. It connects to your team's mailboxes, uses AI to triage and classify incoming mail, and lets agents do the follow-through work — answering, routing, opening tickets, updating CRMs, and assigning tasks to the right people.

A new Thorin tenant gives your organization:

- **Personal and shared agents** that read selected inboxes, follow rules you define, and act on email through connected tools.
- **Switchboard** — a screening surface where each user reviews what their personal agent intends to do before it ships.
- **Skills, Responsibilities, and Work Items** for organizing how agents handle recurring work and how humans pick up what gets handed off.
- **Library and Entities** for the documents, contacts, and structured records agents reference.
- **Integrations and MCP servers** for connecting Salesforce, Linear, GitHub, Notion, Slack, Google Drive, and more.
- **Trust Center** controls — email access policies, privileged-content protection, audit logs, and per-user configuration.
