Thorin is an AI agent platform for work that lives in your inbox. Each user gets a **personal agent** that can read email, run scheduled checks, follow team-wide processes, and hand off tasks to a human reviewer when needed. Admins set up the integrations and rules; everyone else just talks to their agent.

## What you can do with Thorin

- **Triage and reply to email.** Your agent watches your inbox, drafts responses, and surfaces only the messages that need you.
- **Automate recurring work.** Define [responsibilities](/concepts/responsibilities) that fire on a schedule or when an event happens (a new email, a meeting, a doc update).
- **Run shared processes.** Group agents under a [project](/concepts/projects) so a whole team operates from the same playbook with the same data sources.
- **Screen inbound requests.** Put a [Switchboard](/features/switchboard) in front of a busy inbox so a reviewer (you or a delegate) approves what gets through.
- **Track what got done.** Every action lands as a [work item](/concepts/work-items) you can search, filter, and audit.
