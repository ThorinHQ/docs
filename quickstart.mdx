This guide takes a brand-new user from zero to a working personal agent. By the end you'll have signed in, granted Thorin access to your email and calendar, and asked your agent to do something useful.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, you need:

- A Thorin tenant. If your organization isn't onboarded yet, ask your admin or contact Thorin support.
- A Google Workspace account in a domain that's been configured in your tenant's WorkOS setup.
- An admin who has granted Thorin permission to your inbox — typically via [domain-wide delegation](/admin/permissions) or [OAuth](/admin/permissions#oauth).

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Go to your tenant's Thorin URL (for example, `https://app.thorin.com`) and sign in with Google through WorkOS. The first time you sign in, your **personal agent** is provisioned automatically.

    If sign-in fails with a permissions error, your account hasn't been added to the Thorin group in WorkOS yet — ask your admin to add you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your inbox is connected">
    Open the **Home** page. If your agent is ready, you'll see a chat box. If you see "Personal agent not provisioned," wait a minute and refresh — provisioning runs in the background.

    To verify email access, ask your agent something like:

    ```text
    Summarize the last 5 emails in my inbox.
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure notifications and inbox watch">
    Go to **Settings → Notifications** and add a phone number if you want SMS or call alerts. Toggle the channels you want.

    Then open **Settings → Inbox Management** and turn on **Inbox Watch** so your agent reacts to new mail in real time. See [Settings](/features/settings) for what each toggle does.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a responsibility">
    A [responsibility](/concepts/responsibilities) is something your agent does on its own. Open **Responsibilities → New** and create one — for example, a daily 8am summary:

    - **Trigger:** Cron, `0 8 * * *`
    - **Instruction:** "Every weekday at 8am, send me a short Slack message with the most important emails from the last 24 hours."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the result">
    When the responsibility runs, the result shows up as a [work item](/concepts/work-items). Open **Work Items** to see status, output, and the reasoning the agent used.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Next steps

- Browse the [skills catalog](/concepts/skills) and install ones your agent should be able to use.
- Connect a [personal MCP server](/integrations/personal-mcps) if you have private tools you want your agent to call.
- If you handle a lot of inbound email, set up [Switchboard](/features/switchboard) to put a reviewer in front of your inbox.
