This walkthrough takes a brand-new Thorin user from sign-in to a working personal agent in about ten minutes.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, you need:

- A Thorin tenant your admin has provisioned for your organization (you'll receive a sign-in link).
- A Google Workspace account on a domain your admin connected to Thorin.
- Permission to grant Thorin access to your Gmail inbox during sign-in.

If you don't have a tenant yet, ask your admin to follow the [admin onboarding guide](/admin/onboarding).

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Open your tenant URL (typically `https://<your-org>.thorin.com`) and sign in with the SSO provider your admin configured. First-time sign-in walks you through a Google consent screen so Thorin can read and act on your inbox on your behalf.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your personal agent">
    After sign-in, Thorin provisions a **personal agent** scoped to your user. Open **Home** in the sidebar to confirm the agent shows up. Your personal agent is the one that reads your inbox and proposes actions in Switchboard.

    If Home shows a "Personal agent not provisioned" empty state, refresh the page — provisioning runs asynchronously and usually completes within a few seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Switchboard">
    Click **Switchboard** in the sidebar. Switchboard is where your agent shows you the emails it's about to process and what it plans to do with each one. New tenants start with **review-required** mode — nothing ships until you actively approve it.

    See [Switchboard](/concepts/switchboard) for the full review flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install a skill">
    Skills are reusable capabilities — drafting replies, opening Linear issues, summarizing threads — that you snap onto an agent. Open **Skills** in the sidebar and install one from the catalog. Installed skills become available to your personal agent immediately.

    See [Skills](/features/skills) for authoring and installation details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your first automation">
    Open **Automations** in the sidebar and click **New automation**. Pick a trigger — an inbound email matching a filter, a schedule, or a chat message — and write a short instruction telling your agent what to do.

    See [Automations](/features/automations) for trigger types and examples.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect a personal MCP (optional)">
    To give your agent access to your own tools, open a chat with your personal agent, click the **+** icon in the composer, and choose **Connectors → Manage connectors**. Pick a preset (Granola, Notion, Linear, Sentry, or Figma) or add any other MCP server by URL. See [Personal MCPs](/integrations/personal-mcps) — Thorin handles OAuth on your behalf for servers that support it.
  </Step>
</Steps>
